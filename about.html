About Us

Rhythm Rewind is a novel cognitive engagement product
focused on helping Alzheimer's patients minimize
their symptoms through the use of journaling activities
and item matching brain games. In particular, Rhythm Rewind
is focused on memories associated with music to trigger
nostalgia that can help patients connect their disjointed
memories and regain their waning sense of self.
